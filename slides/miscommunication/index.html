Questions to Consider

Do you think most communication is miscommunication?

What's something you enjoy that others
would call noise?

What's the desired signal or experience?
What interferes with it?

When is noise synonymous with "inefficient"?
"Unwanted"? "Meaningless"?

When is noise a form of direct or indirect feedback?

How do people (including you) respond to
that feedback?
