Ownership Effects

Global media are produced by a few
companies (monopolies and oligopolies),
which advertise everywhere.

Content is driven by mass appeal and traffic.

Art, creativity, and facts matter
less than profit and attention.

See Kantar's "2024 Global Top 100 infographic," for instance.
Also consider mergers such as Disney + ABC (1995), Google + YouTube (2006),
Comcast + NBC Universal (2011), and Disney + 21st Century Fox (2019).
