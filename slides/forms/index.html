Models of Comm

Transmission: unidirectional (sender to receiver),
the means, little to no context

Interactive: bidirectional (sender and receiver),
psycho-social relation, some context

Transactional: context is generative,
communicators instead of sender/receiver,
emphasis on norms and culture

The transactional model is also called the cultural or intercultural model of comm.

Models + Forms of Comm

Transmission :: Mass

Interactive :: Intrapersonal, Organizational

Transactional :: Interpersonal, Intrapersonal

Here, I'm suggesting where each model leans. Each form of comm is inevitably present in every model.
